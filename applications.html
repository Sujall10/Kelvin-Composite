Industry Applications

Kelvin Composites materials are designed for performance across a wide range of sectors.

Marine

Our composites are used in hulls, decks, and components for superior corrosion resistance and lightweight durability.

Industrial

Ideal for heavy-duty environments, our composites are used in pipelines, tanks, and structural panels.

Aerospace

Lightweight and high-strength materials for aircraft interiors, structural parts, and thermal insulation components.

Automotive

Enhancing fuel efficiency and performance in vehicles with lightweight body panels and under-hood components.

Construction

Durable composite materials for bridges, facades, and infrastructure that resist weather and corrosion.

Energy

From wind turbine blades to battery casings, our composites are shaping the future of sustainable energy.
