Our Company

Innovative composite solutions for demanding applications

Kelvin Composites is a leading manufacturer of high-performance composite materials, serving industries worldwide with innovative solutions that combine strength, durability, and lightweight properties.

Founded in 1998, we have grown from a small research facility to a global provider of advanced composite materials, with manufacturing facilities in North America and Europe.

Our team of materials scientists, engineers, and manufacturing specialists work together to develop custom solutions for our clients' most challenging requirements.

Our Mission

To deliver innovative, high-performance composite solutions that enable our clients to push the boundaries of what's possible in their industries.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in advanced composite materials, recognized for our technical excellence and commitment to sustainability.

Our Values

Innovation, Quality, Integrity, and Customer Focus guide everything we do at Kelvin Composites.

Our History

Two decades of innovation in composite materials

1998

Company Founded

Kelvin Composites was founded in Boston, MA with a focus on marine composite materials.

2005

First Major Contract

Secured our first major contract with a leading boat manufacturer, establishing our reputation in the marine industry.

2012

European Expansion

Opened our first European manufacturing facility in Bremen, Germany to serve growing international demand.

2018

Aerospace Division

Launched our aerospace composites division to serve the growing demand for lightweight aircraft components.

2023

Sustainability Initiative

Introduced our eco-friendly composite line made with recycled materials and sustainable manufacturing processes.
